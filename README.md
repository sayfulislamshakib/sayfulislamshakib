## About Me
Born and raised in Bangladesh, I’ve always loved working "behind the scenes." Whether it was managing lights and music for school plays or designing props, I found joy in doing the work that helped others on stage shine. Today, that same passion drives my design career. I am motivated by ambiguity and love to simplify complex problems through intentionality. My goal is to empower others through thoughtful designs executed with a high level of craft.

“I want to help other people and businesses shine and do better work.”
